What is PixSort?
PixSort is an iPhone app that uses AI to automatically organize your camera roll into smart albums — by milestone, person, and moment. No manual sorting required.
Will it work on my existing photos?
Yes — all of them. PixSort scans your entire camera roll, even photos from years ago, and organizes them into albums automatically.

Never. PixSort only organizes — it never deletes anything without your explicit approval. Your photos are always safe.
