What is PixSort?
PixSort is an iPhone app that uses AI to automatically organize your camera roll into smart albums — by subject, date, location, and more. No manual sorting required.

Yes. PixSort organizes both photos and videos. It's built specifically for creators who shoot a lot of B-roll and need to find specific clips fast.

Never. PixSort only organizes — it never deletes anything without your explicit approval. The Clean Up feature shows you what to delete, and you decide.
