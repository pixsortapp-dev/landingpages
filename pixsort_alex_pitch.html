What PixSort does

Three things. One app.
No cloud. No sharing. No nonsense.

PixSort lives entirely on the iPhone. AI organizes the camera roll into smart albums you can actually use - by subject, location, person, vibe - and pushes them straight back to Apple Photos so any editing app can pick them up.

01
Find it.

Search the entire camera roll by what's actually in the photo. "Beach sunset." "Gym mirror selfie." "B-roll, Bali, motorbike." Three taps, three seconds.

02
Sort it.

AI builds smart albums automatically - and lets the creator define their own categories. The AI fills them. Screenshots, duplicates, and junk get separated from the keepers.

03
Send it.

Push any sorted album to Apple Photos so it shows up natively in CapCut, InShot, Final Cut, or whatever the editor uses. No exporting. No links. No cloud.
